About Us

We are a family of pediatricians, pediatric nurses and parents. We've had sick kids. Having the right dose at the right time has been a valueable tool to help our children feel better, fevers to resolve, and our kids to get rest. Dose ranges and fumbling with calculators can be difficult even for parents who are nurses and pediatricians. We wanted to create a FREE and USABLE tool that would help caregivers easily calculate and access the appropriate dose for their children.

We want to start a company that aims to improve the quality of healthcare by starting with, engaging and empowering the "patient" aka you with all the available current and evidence based recommendations. We plan to re-invest any revenue into the company. We're also committed to you. After covering the cost of operating, we also commit to invest one dollar for every dollar generated from ad revenue into organizations aimed at improving healthcare.
